Quick Biryani [Vegan]

Description:

A simple, quick, and vegan recipe for a Biryani.
This recipe is not authentic, and instead is a modified version of the indian Biryani.
Perfect as a quick lunch or dinner. Done in 10 minutes without compromising deliciousness!

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup of cooked basmati rice
  • 1 tablespoon neutral oil or vegan butter
  • 1 teaspoon cumin
  • 1 teaspoon turmeric
  • 1 teaspoon garam masala
  • 1 teaspoon red pepper
  • 1 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1 teaspoon salt (to taste)
  • 1 tablespoon tomato paste
  • 1 small onion, diced
  • 4 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon of fresh or ground ginger
  • 1 medium potato, diced and cooked
  • Your choice of vegetables, beans, greens, etc.
    Note: Make sure that any hard vegetable is already cooked.
  • OPTIONAL: 1/4 cup coconut milk OR vegan yogurt

Steps:

  1. Heat the oil in a saucepan or pot.
  2. Add in the onion, garlic, ginger, spices, and tomato paste. Be quick and stir constantly to avoid burning
  3. Add the potatoes and vegetables of your choice.
  4. Add a little of water gradually to avoid burning until the onions are cooked, then add enough water to make it sauce-like.
    You can add coconut milk or yogurt at this step instead of the water or with it.
  5. Taste and add salt if needed, then add the cooked rice on top of it.
  6. Cover and let cook on low heat until the liqued has evaporated.
  7. Enjoy!
